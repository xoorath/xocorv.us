Getting started

Getting an account

Accounts are created by invitation. Reach out to xo corvus to request access. Once your account is ready you will receive a username and password to use across all three services.

Watching content

Visit jellyfin.xocorv.us and sign in with your credentials. From there you can browse and stream the full library directly in your browser.

Requesting movies and shows

Visit seerr.xocorv.us and sign in with your credentials. To request something:

  1. Search for the movie or show by name.
  2. Open its page and click Request.
  3. That’s it — it will be added to the library when available.

Playing retro games

Visit romm.xocorv.us and sign in with your credentials. From there you can browse the game library by console or search by title. Games can be played directly in the browser or downloaded to use with an emulator of your choice.

Apps for phone and tablet

Jellyfin has free official apps for mobile and tablet. After installing, point the app at jellyfin.xocorv.us and sign in with your credentials.

  1. iPhone & iPad — Jellyfin on the App Store
  2. Android — Jellyfin on Google Play

Jellyfin also works in any web browser without installing anything.
